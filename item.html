eRepublik
eRepublik is a web game with the Massive Online Social Strategy Game genre. Yes, here the players will focus more on social life and interact with fellow citizens. You can't expect a game of skill fights like a DoTA in this game. The reason is, eRepublik players can only carry out their daily lives. There is also such a thing as leveling here. Each player certainly has to level up his character by collecting experience points. Also, eRepublik is a real time game, which means you can't speed up your time like playing The Sims. T-Rex Chrome
Have you ever been browsing and then suddenly lost your connection? It must be very annoying and annoying, right? Well, maybe for you Chrome users, this annoyance and resentment can be a little relieved by the presence of T-Rex Chrome. Yes, T-Rex Chrome is a mini game provided by Chrome when the internet connection suddenly drops. The gameplay is very simple, you just have to make the T-rex run and also jump over the obstacles. You can get the highest score if the T-rex you use runs very far. Uniquely, T-Rex Chrome can not only be played when the internet connection is lost, you know. The reason is, you can visit the Chrome web to play this T-Rex Chrome. If you are interested in playing it, you can click here directly.

Bloons Tower Defense 5
Bloons Tower Defense 5 is one of the games that we recommend to fill the time gap. You can play this simple game via PC. No need to download and install the game, because Bloons Tower Defense 5 is already available on the web and you can play it immediately. The gameplay is very easy, you just need to focus on popping the balloons that cross the road. Well, the way to pop it is by placing the monkey on the side of the road. The monkeys that you place will automatically fire off the passing balloons. Apart from monkeys, you can also set other traps to pop balloons.
